## OVERALL DIRECTION

A 60-second cinematic commercial for **Mitype** — the platform where people find their type of people. Five vignettes. Two human beings per vignette. Every pair from a different cultural background. Each vignette pulled from a different Mitype category. Between each vignette, a single continuous-feeling drone move (ascend, swing/orbit, push through, descend) sews the next world together so the entire film reads as one breathing organism. Final beat: brand resolve and offer card.

**Casting note for every scene:** real-looking humans, varied body types, varied ages 20s–40s, every pair representing a different ethnic and cultural background (Black, East Asian, Indigenous/Native, South Asian, Latin American, Middle Eastern, Pacific Islander, mixed, white). Wardrobe: contemporary, considered, lived-in — never costumey.

## SCENE 1 — FITNESS & OUTDOORS (Rock Climbers)

```
[Master Style Block prepended]

Wide cinematic establishing aerial shot, sunrise golden hour, FPV cinematic drone descending from above a cathedral-scale red sandstone cliff face in the American Southwest desert (Moab, Utah aesthetic). Two rock climbers — a Black woman in her late 20s with locs tied back, and a Filipino man in his 30s — are roped together mid-ascent on a vertical wall, 200 feet above the canyon floor. The woman reaches the top first, plants her hand on the ledge, looks down with a wide grin, then extends her arm down to her partner. He grasps her forearm. She pulls. He summits. They embrace standing on the edge as the sun crests the canyon rim behind them.

Drone executes a slow 270-degree orbit around the embracing pair, lens just barely catching anamorphic flare from the cresting sun. Switch to 120fps slow motion as their hands clasp. Push in to a tight medium of their faces — pure, earned joy. Dust on their skin, chalk on their hands, real exertion in their breath. No dialogue. Ambient: wind, distant raven, the faint creak of carabiners.

Shot on ARRI Alexa 35, 32mm anamorphic, ND 1.2, deep golden hour key, natural fill from canyon wall bounce. Color: warm amber highlights, deep teal canyon shadows. 4K UHD, 2.39:1.
```

**Transition out:** Drone breaks the orbit and rockets vertically upward, leaving the climbers small below. As it climbs into the high blue, the frame whip-pans through a cloud — the cloud morphs into the next sky.

## SCENE 2 — TRAVEL & CULTURE / HOBBIES (Stargazers — Astronomers + Adventurers)

```
[Master Style Block prepended]

Emerging from a high cirrus cloud, the drone descends through deep indigo blue-hour twilight into the Atacama high desert of Chile. The Milky Way arcs ceiling-to-horizon overhead — astrophotography-grade galactic core visible, Magellanic clouds, no light pollution. On a flat obsidian-black volcanic ridge, a Brazilian-Indigenous woman in her 30s and a Lebanese man in his late 20s sit shoulder to shoulder on a wool blanket, a vintage Celestron telescope beside them. She is pointing at a constellation; he is following her finger and laughing softly. A thermos of tea steams between them. Two mugs.

Drone descends and settles into a slow lateral tracking dolly past them at eye level, then rises behind them so we see what they see — the entire galactic core. Hold on the silhouette of the two heads against the Milky Way for three seconds. Cut in on a 65mm telephoto medium close-up: their breath fogs in the cold air, starlight catches in her eyes.

Shot on ARRI Alexa 35 with full-frame mode, Sigma Cine 35mm T1.5 wide open, ISO 3200 dual-native, real exposure of the night sky composited in-camera style (no fake stars). Color: deep cobalt and violet shadows, warm ember from distant hand-warmer light. 4K UHD, 2.39:1.

Quiet ambient: wind, the soft tick of the telescope mount, her whisper "there it is."
```

**Transition out:** Drone lifts straight up into the Milky Way. The galaxy's core glow blooms and washes the frame white. The white pulls back to become a ring light.

## SCENE 3 — DIGITAL & CONTENT (Influencers / Content Creators)

```
[Master Style Block prepended]

The white bloom resolves into a circular LED ring light. Pull back to reveal a vibrant rain-slick alley in Seoul at night — neon Hangul signage in pink, cyan, and electric blue reflects off wet asphalt. A Black-Caribbean woman in her mid 20s with a high pony, paired with a Korean man in his late 20s in oversized streetwear, are mid-choreography. Their iPhone is mounted on a Manfrotto tripod, recording. They hit a synced pose, glance at each other, crack up, reset. The phone screen is visible — their TikTok / Reels app is live.

Drone executes a smooth low-altitude push-in, rising from ankle height to a circular orbit at chest height. Switch to 120fps as they spin in unison. The wet pavement reflects everything. Volumetric haze catches the neon. Cyberpunk-meets-real-life. As the orbit completes, the man casually says directly to camera, mid-laugh:

"Mitype was built with creators in mind."

He shrugs and they go back to dancing. Drone pulls up and back, the alley shrinking below.

Shot on ARRI Alexa 35, 25mm anamorphic, T2, practical neon as key light, kicker from the ring light, fill from wet pavement bounce. Color: saturated magenta-cyan duotone with naturalistic skin retention. 4K UHD, 2.39:1, 120fps slow-mo on the spin beats, conformed to 24fps timeline.
```

**Transition out:** Drone climbs above the alley and rotates over the city skyline. The neon dissolves into copper-lit pendant lights of an intimate restaurant kitchen.

## SCENE 4 — FOOD & LIFESTYLE (Chefs)

```
[Master Style Block prepended]

The drone descends through a skylight into a moody, copper-lit chef's kitchen — exposed brick, hanging dried herbs, polished steel. A Mexican woman chef in her 30s and a Punjabi-Indian man in his late 20s stand across a steel pass from each other. She is finishing a plate with tweezers, micro-greens and yuzu gel; he is hand-torching the surface of a tuna crudo, controlled flame licking the protein. They look up at each other, wordless, share a single nod of mutual respect, and slide the plate forward together.

Drone executes a low-angle slow dolly across the pass, capturing the flame in slow motion (120fps), the steam, the tweezers, the gloss of olive oil hitting hot metal. Tilt up to catch their faces lit by the practical flame — warm, cinematic Caravaggio lighting. Then a fluid arc upward to a top-down 90-degree God's-eye view of the finished plate. Hold for two seconds.

Shot on ARRI Alexa 35, Cooke 50mm anamorphic, T2.3, practical flame and tungsten pendants only, no synthetic light. Color: deep cognac warmth, shadows crushed but legible, food saturated to commercial-grade. 4K UHD, 2.39:1.

Ambient: the hiss of the torch, the clink of tweezers on porcelain, jazz vinyl crackling faintly in the background.
```

**Transition out:** Steam from the plate rises and fills the frame. Drone follows the steam upward — the steam dissolves into low-hanging clouds drifting past a city skyline at dusk.

## SCENE 5 — MUSIC (Musicians)

```
[Master Style Block prepended]

The drone emerges from clouds and descends toward a Brooklyn rooftop at deep magic hour — sky gradient burnt orange to violet to ultramarine. The Manhattan skyline rakes the horizon. A Pacific Islander woman in her late 20s with a vintage Gibson J-45 acoustic guitar sits on an overturned milk crate; an Iranian-American man in his early 30s stands beside her with a handheld dynamic mic on a cable, eyes closed, mid-vocal. She strums. He sings. A small string of warm Edison bulbs crisscrosses overhead, just turning on. Two glasses of natural wine on a low concrete ledge.

Drone executes a slow 360-degree orbit around them at chest height, lens grabbing flares from the Edison bulbs and the last of the sun. Then it pulls back and rises, revealing the full rooftop, then the block, then the borough. The skyline lights begin to twinkle on like stars in a parallel constellation to Scene 2.

Shot on ARRI Alexa 35, Cooke 40mm anamorphic, T2, practical Edison string lights as key, sky as massive natural fill, ARRI SkyPanel S60 just out of frame for soft kicker. Color: amber, magenta, indigo gradient, glowing. 4K UHD, 2.39:1.

Ambient: live unprocessed acoustic guitar, his unprocessed voice — raw, intimate, real. Distant traffic horns far below. Wind in the strings of the J-45.
```

**Transition out:** Drone continues climbing until the rooftop becomes one of thousands of lit windows. Each window across the skyline begins to softly glow brighter, in rhythm. The lights bloom into a wash of warm white.

## ARTLIST.IO WORKFLOW NOTES

1. **Generate each scene as a separate clip** using the per-scene prompts above (each block in the code fence). Most state-of-the-art generators max out around 8–12 seconds per clip — that's perfect for 10-second scenes.
2. **Use Artlist's AI Image-to-Video feature** for tighter consistency on character likeness across the orbit shots — generate one strong reference still per scene, then drive the motion off it.
3. **Use Artlist's Director Mode / camera-control prompt features** for the drone transitions specifically — the orbit, push-in, ascent, descent, and dolly moves should be specified as explicit camera commands in the platform's UI rather than relying on prose alone.
4. **For the ring-light → galaxy → neon transitions**, generate the bridging frames as morphing clips with Artlist's transition AI, or render them as cross-dissolve in your NLE (Premiere / Resolve / Final Cut) — the prompt above describes them so a human editor can finish the move.
5. **Pull the score from Artlist's music library** using the brief above as your search query.
6. **Add the brand card and offer text in post** — generative video models butcher text. Use After Effects or Resolve Fusion for the wordmark, set in the actual Mitype amber `#c8956c`.
