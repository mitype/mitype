# Mitype
### The Creator Connection Platform

---

## The Pitch

Mitype is the creator's social network — a place where musicians, writers, artists, photographers, chefs, athletes, gamers, collectors, and creators of every kind connect based on the craft they actually practice and the things they actually love.

Not for dating. Not for corporate networking. For finding your people.

## What Mitype Does

Every member builds a profile around their craft, picking from over **150 creator categories** spanning the full creative and professional spectrum. Painters, podcasters, anglers, anime fans, tattoo artists, music producers, sports fans, collectors, healthcare workers, skilled trades — everybody fits.

From there, Mitype delivers six core experiences:

**Discover** — Browse real people in your city or across the world, filtered by craft category and ZIP code. Real profiles, not algorithms feeding you what you'll click.

**Connect** — Send connection requests that require approval before messaging opens. Quality over quantity. Zero DM spam.

**Spotlight** — A community-curated feed of actual portfolio work — music, films, photos, writing, art — surfaced and rotated daily.

**Weekly** — A shared creative prompt every week. Members write, members vote, the best rises to the top. A new ritual the platform owns.

**Conversations & Mini-Games** — Approved chats include built-in icebreakers — would-you-rather, two truths and a lie, emoji guessing. Connection feels like play.

**Public profiles** — Every member gets a shareable link, extending their reach outside the app and turning Mitype into a creator's calling card.

## Why Nothing Else Does This

LinkedIn is for jobs. Instagram is for performance. Behance is for portfolios with no networking. Reddit is for posts, not people. Facebook is for the people you already know. Tinder is for dating. TikTok is for an algorithm.

**Mitype is the missing layer between all of them.**

It's the place a guitarist in Brooklyn finds the videographer who shares her aesthetic. Where a chef in Austin finds the food photographer who gets his style. Where a Pokémon collector finds another collector who lives twenty minutes away. Where the things that already define your life become the doorway to the people you're meant to know.

**The wedge:** identity-first, craft-anchored, approval-gated. No noise. No engagement-bait. No dating mechanics. Just members who actually want to find each other.

## The Market

Tinder charges twenty dollars a month and crossed 75 million users. Bumble crossed 50. Discord hit 200 million monthly actives without ever charging a subscription. LinkedIn reached one billion. The market for connection software is one of the largest in technology — and it's still growing.

Mitype is positioned cleanly inside that market with a sharper wedge, a clearer customer, and a price point ($5) priced for accessibility, not gatekeeping.
